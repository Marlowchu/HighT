Our Mission

The mission of Team High T is to disrupt and defeat all enemy forces in the region of Al Mazrah. Our objective is to leave a trail of destruction and be the sole team standing. Team High T will accomplish this mission by outsmarting, out shooting, and out driving all oppositions. All members of Team High T will conduct themselves in accordance with the laws of armed conflict and the highest standards of military professionalism.

Operational Excellence

High T members are trained to think critically and make decisions quickly, often under high pressure or stressful circumstances. They are also trained to follow orders and adhere to a chain of command. High T members are taught to think strategically and plan for various contingencies.
